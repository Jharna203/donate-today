Our Donate Plan

Vital infrastructure has been destroyed. Our plans to collect
public donation to help flood victims. Join with us.

Food for a family

We support to giving food boxes or cans, cookies and crackers in plastic bags, and keep them in air-tight storage containers. Empty opened packages of sugar, dried fruits and nuts into screw-top jars or airtight food storage containers to protect them from pests.

Shelter for a family

Residents of 17 sub-districts have been living amid huge sufferings as the overflowing river water flooded their homesteads. Tents, plastic sheeting, prefabricated buildings, temporary or transitional shelters are urgent requirement for majority of the victim.

Education for a child

Gently used clothing, socks, shoes, bedding, and blankets are almost always in need after a devastating flood. You can also help the children affected by floods by sending them food items and bottled drinking water, books, toys centres and sanitation facility at schools.

Their lives depend on your donation

Many families are stranded and could run out of food within days. Homes and schools as well as vital infrastructure such as power supply lines, water sources and roads are all damaged or underwater.

As markets are flooded and not operating, people have nowhere to buy supplies, so it’s urgent that aid gets through to them.

They need us to suvive

When floods strike, vulnerable children, women, men, and elders are cut off from life-saving medicine, vital communication, and transport. Thousands of toilets and tube wells have already been destroyed, and without adequate sanitation and shelter.

Without vital aid and intervention, so many will continue to suffer beyond the crisis.
